The discovery of exoplanets can bring about many questions and curiosities, We have included two tables to help satisfy these. Our Glossary provides a brief description of some key terms and a link for those interested in a more in depth explanation. The Further Information table provides a variety of videos, categorized from 1 to 3, where 1 is the least technical and 3 the most.

       Our hope is that you use some of these links as a starting point to help further your understanding of this incomprehensible reality we live in known as the universe. What lies beyond our solar system and our galaxy? Is there any other life out there? Is any of it intelligent? How can we possibly traverse such inconceivable distances to find out?  The earth will not remain habitable forever and if we wish to persist as a species, we will need to figure all these things out.
